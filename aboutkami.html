The Vision Intelligence Company

  • Kami Vision is on a path to making vision AI affordable and accessible to all businesses and consumers.
  • We started in 2017 as a consumer platform subsidiary of Yi technologies, a video device manufacturer.
  • Soon we offered ecosystems for building vision AI applications, hardware design for vision and Edge IoT solutions.
  • The growing requirements and demands of our client base motivated us to expand our solutions portfolio further.
  • Today, we have become an independent business unit enabling businesses to derive intelligent insights from surveillance camera footages with the help of end-to-end vision AI solution, edge computing and cloud technologies.
