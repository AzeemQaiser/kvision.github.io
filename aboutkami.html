Our Executive Team

CEO

Yamin Durrani

Yamin Durrani is the CEO of Kami Vision Inc. Yamin joined Kami as VP of Sales and Marketing in 2018. Prior to Kami Vision Yamin spent 5 years at Fairchild Semiconductor where he managed and grew the sales of mobile chip solution. Yamin has over 10 years of experience in B2B sales leadership and product marketing in high-tech companies.

Yamin has an MBA from Santa Clara University, MS Electrical Engineering from Texas A&M University and Bachelors in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, India where he graduated at the top of his class.

CPO

Mukund Rajukumar

A product leader with 20 years of experience building several successful vision technology products and software services for the global market. Prior to Kami, Mukund led product management at Live Planet, Inc to launch their VR content publishing platform. He has 2 patents including one on Intel’s video driver architecture.

He holds an MBA from UC Berkeley Haas School of Business and an MS in Electrical Engineering from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor.

CTO

Ajay Gulati

Dr. Ajay Gulati is a technologist, serial entrepreneur and leads the product design and innovation at Kami Vision. Ajay brings 15 years of experience in building diverse technologies like AI-enabled cameras, a fully managed cloud platform, QoS and management of cloud resources. Ajay built ZeroStack, a cloud startup from inception to a successful exit.

He has been recognized as a prolific inventor and researcher by his peers and has more than a dozen patents to his name. Ajay has a Ph.D. from Rice University and a B.S. from IIT Kharagpur.

Advisor

Gaurang Shah

Gaurang Shah is a proven C-Suite leader with over 25 years of experience in P&L, Operational and Technology Management at Fortune 100 companies. Most recently, he served as the COO of YI Technologies: a Computer Vision company that is committed to deliver Edge Intelligence to consumers and businesses.

Prior to that Gaurang spent 25 years in the Semiconductor industry in various business and product development Executive roles. He earned his BSEE from Michigan Technological University and MSEE from Cornell University.

Advisor

Xinhua Liu

Xinhua Liu is a venture partner of Gaorong Capital Investment, focusing on investment opportunities and growth advisory for SaaS enterprise services and B2B marketplaces. Mr. Liu had over 22 years of management experience with high-tech and internet companies in strategy, growth, operations management and corporate development roles, and led the launch and growth of several blockbuster apps with tens or hundreds of millions of daily active users.
