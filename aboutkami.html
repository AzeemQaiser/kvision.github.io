The Vision Intelligence

Company

  • Kami Vision is on a path to making vision AI affordable and accessible to all businesses and consumers.

  • We started in 2017 as a consumer platform subsidiary of Yi Technologies, a renowned video device manufacturer

  • Soon we offered software ecosystems for building vision AI applications, hardware design for vision devices and Edge IoT solutions.

  • The growing requirements and demands of our client base motivated us to expand our solutions portfolio further.

  • Today, we have become an independent business unit enabling businesses to derive intelligent insights from surveillance camera footages with the help of end-to-end vision AI solutions, edge computing and cloud technologies
