Senior Automation Engineer

Location: California

Job Overview

We are looking for a Sr. Automation Engineer to join our team at Kami Vision. As a Test Automation Engineer, you will have the opportunity to design and develop automated tests using innovative tools and frameworks. You will work closely with developers and Product Management to build and execute automated test scenarios against the Kami Vision apps and IoT Platform. Automated testing is a crucial aspect of our IoT platform and your contributions will directly impact the quality of the Kami's product and various integrations. The automated tests you develop will run as part of our continuous development environment, providing product quality feedback to Development and QA.


Responsibilities for Data Scientist

Hands on experience with testing, and automation in python Collaborate with a small talented team of engineers Automate testing of phone apps, backend using REST APIs Automate UI testing using selinium and other tools Setup, build and maintain CI/CD pipelines using jenkins


Qualifications for Data Scientist

6+ years working with development and QA as SDET Knowledge of REST and API testing/automation Experience with Python and testing framerworks Proven experience in programmatically testing software products and automating those tests Strong analytical and debugging skills BS/MS in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field preferred Experience writing automated tests in a Continuous Integration (CI) and Continuous deployment (CD) systems
