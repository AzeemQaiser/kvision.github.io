Consumer Products

We develop creative designs for vision devices with complete edge and cloud enablement which are manufactured and produced by our global suppliers.

  • Indoor cameras

    With compact design, motion tracking, two way audio, and auto-cruise panoramic view, the indoor cameras designed by Kami Vision are state-of-the-art and perfect for any kind of surveillance requiring smart facial and human recognition without compromising the aesthetics of home interiors.

  • Kami Shield Protection

    The Kami Shield cloud platform is device agnostic, completely private and secure to the core so that users can store their vision data efficiently and without the fear of breaches.

  • Outdoor cameras

    Our outdoor camera designs are sleek and compact to ensure that the aesthetics of our users’ homes remain uncompromised. With two-way audio, wide viewing angle, dual power and wifi extenders our outdoor cameras stay on constant vigilance so that our users can sleep in peace.
